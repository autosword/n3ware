7 features that ship with every site

Everything you need to
own your website.

From a pixel-perfect nav to AI-generated content, n3ware gives small business owners a full editing suite — no developer, no page builder subscription, no compromise.

1

Pixel-perfect navigation,
out of the box.

Every template ships with a fixed-on-scroll primary nav that's responsive by default. Backdrop blur, brand styling, hamburger drawer on mobile, and a structured-data editor so you never touch HTML to change a menu item.

Fixed on scroll Backdrop blur Mobile hamburger Zero HTML
2

Edit your menu like a form,
not like HTML.

Click your nav, open the Edit Nav panel, and drag-to-reorder menu items, swap labels, toggle a CTA button, upload a brand logo. Every change renders instantly to your live nav and persists to cloud storage.

Drag to reorder Logo upload CTA toggle Live preview
3

Theme your whole site
from one panel.

Pick your primary, secondary, and accent colors. Upload a logo and favicon. Choose heading and body fonts from curated pairings. Tune H1 through body paragraph sizes with live sliders. Every site uses the same design tokens so typography stays consistent.

Color tokens Google Fonts Typography sliders Logo & favicon
4

34 drop-in components
for every SMB need.

Heroes, pricing, testimonials, contact, menu, services, team, gallery, FAQ, CTA, stats, and navigation blocks. Each one built with Tailwind, fully responsive, and ready to drop into any page. Pick one, place it, make it yours.

34 blocks Tailwind-built Fully responsive Instant drop
5

Place components exactly
where you want them.

Click a component and your cursor becomes a preview badge. Drop zones light up between existing sections. Click once — the component lands precisely where you dropped it. Undo anytime.

Visual drop zones Preview badge Precise placement Undo / redo
6

Tell Claude what you want.
Get it.

After dropping any component, a prompt window opens. Describe what it should say, upload reference images, hit Generate. Claude rewrites the content in-place — headline, subhead, CTAs, all on-brand — while preserving the layout. Skip if you prefer the default template.

Powered by Claude Image upload On-brand rewrite Skippable
7

Long-form pages get smart
sub-nav for free.

Services, about, portfolio, locations, FAQ, and menu templates ship with a sticky sub-nav that tracks the section you're reading via intersection observer. Swipeable on mobile. Zero configuration.

IntersectionObserver Sticky scroll-spy Swipeable mobile Zero config
