Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

n3ware ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by n3ware LLC, South Kingstown, RI, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website builder platform at n3ware.com.

By using n3ware, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your plaintext password). We may also collect your name if provided.

Site Content

We store the HTML content of the websites you create and edit using our platform. This content is hosted on our servers (Google Cloud / Firestore) and served to your visitors. You retain full ownership of your content.

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our service, including:

  • Pages visited within the n3ware dashboard
  • Features used and actions taken
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Timestamps and session duration

Page View Analytics

We collect anonymized page view data for sites hosted on n3ware, including visitor counts, page paths, and referrer information. This data is attributed to your site and visible in your dashboard analytics.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We collect your email address for billing purposes. We never see or store your credit card numbers — all payment processing is handled by Stripe in a PCI-compliant environment. We retain records of your subscription status, plan type, and billing history.

Uploaded Files

Images and other files you upload to your sites are stored in our cloud storage (Google Cloud Storage). You retain ownership of all uploaded content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the n3ware platform
  • Process transactions and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts)
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve our service
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. Account data and site content are stored in Google Firestore (a NoSQL database). Uploaded files are stored in Google Cloud Storage. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt (cost factor 12+)
  • API authentication via JWT tokens with 7-day expiry
  • Secrets managed via Google Secret Manager (not stored in code)
  • HTTPS enforced for all connections
  • Input sanitization on all user-provided HTML

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Receives your email address and payment information. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • SendGrid — Transactional email delivery (account and billing emails). Receives email addresses only. SendGrid Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — CDN, DNS management, and DDoS protection. Receives domain names and DNS configuration you provide. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — If you connect a GA4 property to your site, anonymized page view data is shared with Google Analytics. This is optional and controlled by you. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — If you use the AI-powered website migration feature, your existing website's HTML is sent to the Anthropic API for analysis and cleanup. This data is processed in accordance with Anthropic's privacy policy and is not retained by Anthropic for training purposes per their data usage policy.

5. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Authentication token — A JWT token stored in localStorage to keep you signed in
  • Session preferences — Remembering your UI preferences (e.g., active dashboard tab)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can clear cookies via your browser settings; doing so will sign you out of n3ware.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services:

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion
  • Site content and revisions: Retained until site deletion or account deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for 90 days by default
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required for financial record-keeping
  • Email logs: Retained for 30 days for deliverability troubleshooting

Payment Failure & Account Suspension

If a subscription payment fails, your account enters a 30-day grace period during which the service remains available. If payment is not resolved within 30 days, your site(s) may be suspended (taken offline). After 60 days of non-payment, your data may be permanently deleted. We will send at least one notice to your registered email address no less than 30 days before any data deletion.

7. Service Discontinuation

If n3ware discontinues its service, we will provide customers with at least 90 days advance notice via email to their registered address. During this period, all data export functionality will remain available so you can download your site HTML, uploaded files, and account data. We are committed to giving you a fair opportunity to migrate your content.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Export: Export your site content and uploaded files at any time from your dashboard
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format (HTML/JSON)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@n3ware.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

n3ware is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@n3ware.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by emailing registered account holders and by posting a notice on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of n3ware after changes constitute acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@n3ware.com
  • Company: n3ware LLC
  • Address: South Kingstown, Rhode Island, USA
