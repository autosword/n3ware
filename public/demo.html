Our Story

A Passion for Wood-Fired Cooking

Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief: the best meals happen when exceptional ingredients meet ancient technique. Our open-fire kitchen is the heart of everything we do.

We partner with over 30 local farms and fisheries, changing our menu with the seasons to bring you the freshest flavors the region has to offer. Every dish tells a story of place, season, and craft.

Executive Chef

Marcus Reid

Culinary Institute of America · 20 years experience

Chef Marcus learned the fundamentals of fire-cooking from his grandfather in coastal Portugal before training at the finest kitchens in Paris, Tokyo, and New York.

"I believe cooking is about respect — for the ingredient, the farmer, and the guest. Every dish should feel like a conversation across time."

"Fire is the oldest cooking tool. We just got better at listening to it."
