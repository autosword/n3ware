A Taste of The Waypoint

Meet the Chef

Chef Marcus Deluca

Chef Marcus grew up just down the road in Peace Dale before heading north to earn his culinary degree at the Cambridge School of Culinary Arts. He spent twelve years honing his craft in Boston's restaurant scene — including seven years as sous chef at the nationally recognized Harborview — before returning to South County to open The Waypoint in 2018.

His approach is simple: use what's local, trust the seasons, and don't get in the way of great ingredients. Every menu changes monthly to reflect what's fresh from Johnston Farm in Hope Valley, Shady Lea Farm in North Kingstown, and the day boats out of Point Judith. "South County raised me," he says. "This restaurant is my way of giving it back."
