Our Story

Rooted in the Season,
Fired by Passion

Ember & Oak was born from a single belief: the best meals come from the best ingredients. We partner with local farms, foragers, and fishmongers to bring you a menu that changes with the seasons.

Our wood-fired kitchen is the heart of everything we do. Every dish passes through the flame, picking up a depth of flavour you simply can't replicate any other way.
