About Me

Born and raised in South County, I've spent the past 15 years helping buyers and sellers navigate the Rhode Island real estate market with confidence. From the coastal neighborhoods of Narragansett to the charming village streets of Wakefield, I know every corner of our community — the hidden gems, the up-and-coming streets, and the homes that hold their value for decades.
